About Brent

Brent Faiyaz is an American singer, songwriter, and record producer. He gained recognition for his soulful and alternative R&B sound. Born on September 19, 1995, in Columbia, Maryland, his full name is Christopher Brent Wood.

Brent Faiyaz first rose to prominence as a member of the musical group Sonder, which also included producers Atu and Dpat. In 2016, he gained wider attention with the release of his solo debut project, "A.M. Paradox." However, he became even more well-known for his contributions to the soundtrack of the popular television series "Insecure" with his single "Crew" in 2017.
